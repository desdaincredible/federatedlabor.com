  1. Q: When will credit be issued for a claim payment?
    A: The Administrator sends approved claims to NTW on a weekly basis. NTW then issues credit to your dealer account.
  2. Q: What are the hours of operation for the claim center?
    A: The claim center is open Monday through Friday 8 am to 8 pm Eastern time and Saturday 9 am to 6 pm Eastern time. They are closed Sundays and major holidays.
  3. Q: Do I dispose of the tire after the claim is approved?
    A: We recommend holding on to the damaged tire until credit is received from NTW.
  4. Q: What do I need from the customer to ensure that I get paid for road hazard claims?
    A: You should obtain a copy of the original invoice for road hazard coverage. You will attach a copy of the original invoice for the purchase of the tires and your subsequent replacement invoice when you submit a claim.
  5. Q: Does this benefit cover tire repairs?
    A: No, this program is for the replacement of the tire only.
  6. Q: Does this program have a roadside assistance benefit?
    A: Yes, there is a benefit for 1 year from the tire purchase date for towing or flat tire changing assistance. The consumer will be reimbursed for up to $75.00 per service. Your customer must call the Administrator to set up the roadside service.
  7. Q: What if my customer is away from my shop and needs service?
    A: If your customer is 25 miles away or further and has a damaged tire, we ask that they contact the warranty center at 1-800-351-8438 to obtain service. We will set up a claim for your customer and look for a participating Federated Local Labor location to send them to.

    If they are within the 25 miles of your location, they must return back to you for service.
  8. Q: How long is the road hazard warranty valid?
    A: This warranty is valid for three years from the date of purchase or until the tire tread reaches 2/32nds of an inch, whichever comes first.
  9. Q: If I need to email or fax paperwork, what is the fax number and email address that I would use?
    A: Fax: 1-855-765-5696 Email: federated@abswarranty.net
  10. Q: Are commercial accounts or trailers covered under the warranty?
    A: No, commercial accounts and trailers are excluded from the program.
